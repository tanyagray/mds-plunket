Giving your baby the best start in life

From pregnancy until your child is ready for school, your family is entitled to plunket care and support.

Your child is a gift, but caring for a child takes a whole lot of new skills.

Nobody expects you to be an instant success at parenting. Some things will come naturally while others may take a little longer. Everyone is different.

Plunket supports parents in many ways. As well as providing services that directly benefit your child, we offer resources and parenting education.

In this section you will find information for parents who are expecting, as well as reliable advice for parents of children under five.

To make it easier to navigate your way through the early years of parenthood, we have developed a publication. Thriving Under Five is given to all Plunket clients.
